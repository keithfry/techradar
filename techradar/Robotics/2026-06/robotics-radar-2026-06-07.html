Robots can enhance manufacturing workers rather than replace them

Robotics possess the capability to augment, rather than wholly replace, human manufacturing labor. Implementing advanced AI, teleoperation technologies, and appropriate safety safeguards are key to realizing this augmentation. Such integration allows robots to enhance the capabilities of current manufacturing workers. This view was stated by one to ONE Holdings' president. The insights were initially published on The Robot Report.
