Safely Deploying ML Models to Production: A/B, Canary, Interleaved, and Shadow Testing

A thorough guide to four controlled rollout strategies for deploying new ML models to production environments. A/B testing compares models on split traffic; canary releases expose a small user segment first; interleaved testing mingles both models' results for the same user; shadow mode runs the new model in parallel without surfacing its outputs. Each approach helps catch unexpected failures before they impact users at scale.
