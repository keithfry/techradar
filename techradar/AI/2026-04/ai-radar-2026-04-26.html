AI Progress Outpacing Organizational Readiness in The Workplace Today

AI progress is outpacing organizational readiness as technological advancements are arriving faster than companies can absorb them, with models capable of reasoning, searching, coding, summarizing, and operating across long chains of tasks. Companies often want AI due to fear of falling behind, but lack a clear understanding of their work processes, clean process maps, reliable ownership, strong feedback loops, and shared definitions of good execution. The knowledge that exists is stored in people, not systems, making organizational translation the most important AI work happening inside companies today.
