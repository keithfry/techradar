Palm Garden AI develops Coherence Guard relational decision layer for human-facing robots

Palm Garden AI is developing Coherence Guard, a platform-agnostic relational decision layer for human-facing robots. This layer evaluates whether proposed robot actions are relationally coherent within a real human environment, considering factors like timing, proximity, and emotional tone. It functions as an evaluation layer above existing perception and planning systems to guide socially appropriate actions, such as respectful withdrawal when a person shows discomfort. The technology is designed to be complementary to formal safety systems, focusing on relational coherence rather than replacing them. Palm Garden AI is working towards integrating this layer with various robotics providers through simulation-first pathways.
