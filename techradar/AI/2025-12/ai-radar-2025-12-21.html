1. NVIDIA AI Releases Nemotron 3: A Hybrid Mamba Transformer MoE Stack for Long Context Agentic AI

RSS • 2025-12-20T20:34:30.000Z

NVIDIA’s Nemotron 3 model family introduces a hybrid Mamba Transformer Mixture of Experts (MoE) architecture designed for agentic AI applications requiring efficient long-context reasoning, supporting context windows up to 1 million tokens. The three variants—Nano, Super, and Ultra—scale from 30B to 500B parameters with sparse activation to control inference costs, while Super and Ultra add latent MoE and multi-token prediction for improved specialization and decoding speed. Trained on 25 trillion tokens and leveraging NVFP4 4-bit precision, Nemotron 3 offers both open weights and full-stack tools, facilitating integration with RAG, PEFT, LoRA, and frameworks like LangChain and LangGraph. This release advances multi-agent systems and large workspace reasoning critical in legal, code, and complex planning domains.
