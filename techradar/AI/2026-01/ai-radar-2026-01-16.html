RSS • 2026-01-15T21:12:26.000Z

KVzap uses lightweight surrogate models to predict the importance of cache entries based on hidden states, enabling near-lossless 2x-4x compression of the KV cache with minimal compute overhead (~1.1% FLOPs). This adaptive pruning significantly reduces memory consumption during inference, improving batch sizes and latency without sacrificing accuracy on long context benchmarks like RULER, LongBench, and AIME25.
