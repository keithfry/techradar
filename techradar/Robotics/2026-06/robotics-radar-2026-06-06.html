🎓 How to encourage smarter AI use in the classroom

Generative AI tools have entered classrooms, initially surprising both students and educators who struggled with integration. While organizations encourage adoption, teachers face confusion regarding clear guidelines for use. One approach involves training staff on general AI usage while emphasizing the technology's limitations concerning inaccuracies and bias. Educators are currently utilizing AI primarily for lesson planning and creating materials, though feedback generation remains problematic due to concerns about quality and privacy. Some instructors, instead of relying on AI help, challenge students to reflect critically on how tools like large language models can or cannot teach essential skills.

AWR standardizes CNC machine tending with OnRobot electric grippers to speed changeovers

Automation Within Reach (AWR) is standardizing CNC machine tending using OnRobot electric grippers. This addresses the need for adaptability in high-mix, low-volume manufacturing environments. Traditional pneumatic systems limited modern automation capabilities due to their lack of intelligence. Electric grippers provide the necessary flexibility for frequent changes between different part sizes. The technology aims to improve operational efficiency and profitability in machine shops.
