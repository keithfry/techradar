Full-Body Dynamic Safety for Robot Manipulators: 3D Poisson Safety Functions for CBF-Based Safety Filters

A novel approach to full-body dynamic safety for robot manipulators is presented, utilizing 3D Poisson Safety Functions (PSFs) with Control Barrier Function (CBF)-based safety filters. The method samples the manipulator surface at a prescribed resolution and synthesizes a globally smooth CBF by solving Poisson's equation, yielding a single safety function for the entire environment. This framework ensures collision avoidance for the entire robot surface by guaranteeing safety in the buffered region of sampled points. It is validated on a 7-degree-of-freedom manipulator in dynamic environments.
