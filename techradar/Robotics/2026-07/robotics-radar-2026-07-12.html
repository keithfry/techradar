Why robotics teams need virtual gyms before deployment

Robots can gain significant benefits by utilizing "virtual gyms" prior to real-world deployment. These simulations are designed to help robots adapt to variability found in their operating environments and tasks. Such training helps bridge the critical gap known as sim-to-real transfer. By practicing in virtual settings, teams can improve robotic performance robustness before physical deployment.
