The proposed Q-function maps state-action pairs to a long-term safety score based on visibility, recognizability, and graspability criteria, enabling recovery from unsafe actions through gradient ascent. Dynamics Aware Quadrupedal Locomotion via Intrinsic Dynamics Head

Researchers propose a novel training framework for quadrupedal locomotion that enables the Control Policy to understand physical dynamics through an Intrinsic Dynamics Head. The ID Head learns state-to-torque dynamics alongside the Control Policy, promoting more predictable dynamical behavior. Simulation experiments show improved efficiency and smoother policies, with real-robot experiments demonstrating significant gains in torque efficiency, action rate, mechanical power, and safe torque occupancy.
