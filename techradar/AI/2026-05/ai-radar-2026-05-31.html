ROS Discourse General: Show ROS: BAGEL – A zero-install, browser-native 3D visualizer and scrubber for MCAP, db3, and ROS1 bags

BAGEL (BAG ExpLoration) is an open-source, fully static web application that allows users to inspect, scrub, and visualize ROS 1 and ROS 2 bag files locally in their browser with zero installation and configuration. BAGEL uses a client-side architecture and utilizes web workers and streaming chunk decoders to handle heavy robotics telemetry data directly in the browser thread. The tool supports multi-format ingestion, including ROS2 `.mcap` and legacy ROS1 `.bag` files, as well as TF-aware 3D visualization of SLAM maps and remote URL streaming via HTTP Range.
